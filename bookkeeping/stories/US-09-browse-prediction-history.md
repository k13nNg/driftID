# US-09 — Browse past identifications

**Persona:** Used-car shopper  
**Theme:** History & revisiting  
**Introduced:** S002

**As a** used-car shopper, **I want** to see a list of my past identifications, **so that** I can scan what I've already checked at a glance.

**Acceptance criteria:**
- [ ] History is reachable from the History tab (US-07)
- [ ] Entries are ordered most-recent-first
- [ ] Each entry shows a thumbnail, top make/model, confidence, and when it was identified
- [ ] An empty-history state explains how entries get added
