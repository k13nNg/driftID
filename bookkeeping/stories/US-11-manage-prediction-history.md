# US-11 — Manage and clear history

**Persona:** Car enthusiast  
**Theme:** History & revisiting  
**Introduced:** S002

**As a** car enthusiast, **I want** to remove individual entries or clear my whole history, **so that** I can keep the list relevant and control what's stored on my device.

**Acceptance criteria:**
- [ ] A single history entry can be deleted
- [ ] The entire history can be cleared in one action, with a confirmation step
- [ ] Deletions remove the data from browser-local storage immediately
- [ ] After clearing, the empty-history state is shown
